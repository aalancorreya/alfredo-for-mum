Recipes for Mum

Written down so I don't have to keep ringing.

scroll for recipes

This is a small set of recipes I'm writing down for Mum — the ones she's asked me for, and the ones I want to remember her teaching me. Some of them are mine now and some of them will always be hers, and the difference matters less than I used to think.

I'll add to this slowly. A new recipe goes up when one is worth keeping — not before. Each is a love letter that happens to contain instructions, and they're meant to be read at least as much as they're meant to be cooked.
